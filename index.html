Sunrise

Our planet earth revolves around the sun. The sun's light gives rise to day and night; its light is the source of the energy needed by all living creatures. Therefore, mankind has marvelled about the nature of light from time immemorable. Its power was deemed so immeasurable that only a god could have been at the origin of light. Thus, the bible describes the genesis of the world by the words:

In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth
And the earth was without form, and void; ...
And God said, Let there be light, and there was light.
God saw that the light was good,
and he separated the light from the darkness.

We shall briefly explore mankinds attempts to make sense of light.
